About The Unicorn Kitchen

The Unicorn Kitchen was named because since having our daughter everything is unicorns, whether this is because she loves them or we think they are adorable with her cannot yet be proved (she does indeed look adorable with them).

The Unicorn Kitchen is run by fiance's Debbie and Dave who wanted a permanent place to store their recipes, however we are sure other things will appear on here as time goes on.

Debbie is a full time stay at home Mum to their daughter, and Dave is a full time Lead Software Engineer.

Dave has always been the more skilled in cooking, whereas Debbie's skills in the past have been lacking to say the least. She once gave Dave food poisioning by serving an uncooked pizza, served cinnamon potato wedges because she forgot the name of the spice but knew it began with c, and even managed to grill brownies (she was following an American recipe and had the oven on grill as she didn't understand the temperatures were in different measurements.) Debbie has vowed to improve her cooking skills now the two have a daughter, and Dave agrees and is still willing to be the tester before their daughter has anything.
